Welcome to mayday

mayday is a funny, beautifully designed mobile game featuring
- a Bavarian-style theme with Munich skyline
- several game characters to choose from
- great sound effects and background music (device music allowed as well)
- and many more levels to come!

Collect the edelweiss flowers!

How to play:
- tap the screen to fly
- avoid all obstacles
- collect hearts, pretzels and beer along the way.
